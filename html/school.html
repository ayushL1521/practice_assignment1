ARCHANA VIDHYA SANKUL - HIGH SCH was established in 1994 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided

It is located in Urban area. It is located in SURAT CORPO. block of SURAT district of Gujrat
The school consists of Grades from 9 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.

All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall.
