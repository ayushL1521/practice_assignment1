Welcome to MIT WORL PEACE UNIVERSITY.

MIT-WPU is a private university and MIT World Peace University, through about 32 schools of study, offers undergraduate, postgraduate, Diploma, Certificate and other courses to students across the Management, Engineering, Design, and other streams.
MIT Pune is a part of MAEER MIT Group of Institutions.

MIT-WPU formerly known as MIT Pune is the epicenter for imparting value-based education for the promotion of universal culture of peace and welfare among youth.
